Welcome to The Perfect Cut barbershop

Since 2010, it has been our mission to offer an exceptional experience for you and your family. We are located in the heart of Nepean, College Square. Come see our expert barbers at 1980 Baseline Road [inside the Loblaw] to find out what all the buzz is about.

Top notch stylist

We pride ourselves on our detailed cuts and clean shaves. From gentlemen's cuts to razor fades, comb-overs and children haircuts, we do it all..

Experience

We are welcoming neighborhood barbershop, ready to provide you with the perfect cut and service. With years of experience and continuing education, our dedicated staff is ready to serve your beauty needs.

Quality

We offer an array of professional haircut services to our customers of all ages. We guarantee that you will be leaving looking and feeling your best. We also carry a full range of professional haircare products.
